Shipping Information

At Champion Sports, we strive to make your shopping experience as smooth as possible. Here's everything you need to know about our shipping process:

Processing Time

  • Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days.
  • Orders placed on the weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

Shipping Options

We offer the following shipping options:

Standard Shipping Delivery within 5-10 business days.
Express Shipping Delivery within 2-4 business days. (+$10)
Overnight Shipping Delivery by next day if ordered before 4pm CST. (+$15)

Shipping Costs

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on your location and the weight of your order. Free standard shipping is available for orders over $100.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, we'll send you a confirmation email with a tracking number so you can monitor its journey to your doorstep.

Delivery Locations

We currently ship to addresses within the United States. Unfortunately, we do not ship to P.O. boxes or international addresses at this time.

Questions?

If you have any questions about shipping or need help with your order, please reach out to us here.

Thank you for choosing Champion Sports! We're excited to get your order to you.
