ABOUT US

Welcome to Champion Sports. We're more than just a sports equipment store - we're your neighbors, teammates, and biggest fans. Located right in the heart of Iowa, our family-owned business was born out of a passion for sports and a desire to support our community. We know what it's like to lace up for a big game, train for a personal best, or just enjoy a pickup game with friends. That's why we're here - to make sure you have the gear and guidance you need to play your best. Whether it's helping a young athlete find their first pair of cleats or fitting a seasoned player with the perfect gear, we love being part of your sports journey. When you walk into our store, you're not just a customer - you're part of the Iowa Sports Gear family. We're proud to provide not only the best equipment but also advice, encouragement, and a friendly smile. Thank you for letting us be part of your story. Let's keep chasing those goals and making memories - together!
